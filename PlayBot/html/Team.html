Research Ethics

Information Sheet For Participants

You are invited to participate in a project supervised and conducted by Dr YANG Weipeng, an Assistant Professor of the Department of Early Childhood Education at The Education University of Hong Kong.

The introduction of the research

+
A) What does the research involve?
  • We will design a series of play-based programming activities that are developmentally appropriate and culturally responsive for Hong Kong kindergarten children.
  • We will evaluate the impact of the programming interventions on early childhood development.
  • We will train teachers about how to provide early programming education.
B) Why were you chosen for this research?
  • Your kindergarten has kindly agreed to participate in this study.
  • The target age group of children for learning programming in this study is 5-6 years old.
  • Your child is in one of the K3 classrooms in this kindergarten/You are teaching K3 children in this kindergarten.

Information for Teacher Participants

+
A) How many participants will be included in this study?

All the K3 classes, with all the teachers (approximately 24) and children (approximately 360) from these classes, will be included as research participants.

B) Procedure of the research
  • Teachers from K3 classes will receive two 2-hour workshops during two consecutive Saturdays.
  • The participation period will last 10 weeks, of which 2 weeks are for teacher training and child assessment and 8 weeks for curriculum activities (intervention).
  • K3 teachers will need to conduct play-based programming activities or teacher-led programming instruction with selected classes.

Information for Parent Participants

+

The methodology of the research is similar to teacher participants, with specific considerations for children's assessment.

  • Your child will be assessed in a friendly manner to know the status of computational thinking and engagement in robot programming.
  • The assessments with your child will be conducted by a well-trained Research Assistant and happen in a quiet, public area of the kindergarten.

The potential risks of the research

+

The study will present no more than minimal risk to the participants.

  • All Research Assistants will be well-trained to provide comfortable assessment experiences for the children.
  • All participation in the project is voluntary. Teachers/Parents/Children have no career/study risks in withdrawing from this study at any time without negative consequences.

If you would like to obtain more information about this study, please contact Dr YANG Weipeng by email at wyang@eduhk.hk.

研究操守

參與者須知表

您受邀參與由香港教育大學幼兒教育學系助理教授楊偉鵬博士主持並協助執行的研究計劃。

研究簡介

+
A) 研究涉及什麼？
  • 我們將為香港幼稚園幼兒設計一系列符合發展和文化反應的編程活動。
  • 我們將評估編程干預對早期兒童發展的影響。
  • 我們將為教師提供早期編程教學的培訓。
B) 為什麼選擇你參與這項研究？
  • 貴校已同意參與本研究。
  • 本研究中學習編程的目標兒童年齡為5-6歲。
  • 貴子女在這間幼稚園的K3班／您在這間幼稚園任教K3的兒童。

給教師參加者的資訊

+
A) 研究方法

所有K3班級，以及這些班級的所有教師（大約24位）和兒童（大約360位），都將作為研究參與者。

B) 研究程序
  • K3班級的教師將在連續兩個週六接受兩次2小時的工作坊培訓。
  • 參與期將持續10週，其中2週用於教師培訓和兒童評估，8週用於課程活動（干預）。
  • K3教師需要在選定的班級進行遊戲化編程活動或教師指導的編程教學。

給家長參加者的資訊

+

研究方法與教師參與者相似，並特別考慮兒童評估。

  • 貴子女將以友好的方式進行評估，以了解計算思維能力發展和機器人編程的參與度。
  • 與貴子女的評估由受訓良好的研究助理在幼稚園寧靜的公共區域進行。

參與期間有可能面對的風險及不適

+

這項研究對參與者的風險極低。

  • 所有研究助理將受到良好培訓，為兒童提供舒適的評估體驗。
  • 所有參與計劃均屬自願性質。教師/家長/兒童可隨時退出研究，不會有任何負面影響。

如欲了解更多有關這項研究的信息，請聯繫楊偉鵬博士：wyang@eduhk.hk。
