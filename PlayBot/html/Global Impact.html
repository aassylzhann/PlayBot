Global Impact

The Worldwide Reach of PlayBot

Our curriculum is making a positive impact on early childhood education across the globe.

Educational Innovation

PlayBot is revolutionizing how computational thinking is taught in early childhood education, with implementation in over 50 schools across Asia and growing global interest.

Research Contributions

Our research has been presented at international conferences and published in leading academic journals, advancing the field of early childhood technology education worldwide.

Bridging Digital Divides

By providing screen-free access to coding concepts, PlayBot helps bridge digital divides, making computational thinking accessible to children from diverse backgrounds and environments.

PlayBot 的全球影響力

我們的課程正在對全球幼兒教育產生積極影響。

教育創新

PlayBot 正在革新幼兒教育中計算思維的教學方式，目前已在亞洲超過 50 所學校實施，並引起全球越來越多的關注。

研究貢獻

我們的研究成果已在國際會議上展示並發表於主要學術期刊，推動了全球幼兒科技教育領域的發展。

消除數字鴻溝

通過提供無屏幕的編碼概念學習方式，PlayBot 幫助消除數字鴻溝，使來自不同背景和環境的兒童都能接觸到計算思維教育。
