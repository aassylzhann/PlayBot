Global Impact

The Worldwide Reach of PlayBot

Our curriculum is making a positive impact on early childhood education across the globe.

Educational Innovation

PlayBot is revolutionizing how computational thinking is taught in early childhood education, with implementation in over 50 schools across Asia and growing global interest.

Research Contributions

Our research has been presented at international conferences and published in leading academic journals, advancing the field of early childhood technology education worldwide.

Key Research Areas

  • Computational thinking development in early childhood
  • Robot-assisted learning and playful programming
  • Screen-free technology education approaches
  • Cross-cultural implementation of coding education

Academic Collaborations

PlayBot curriculum is developed in collaboration with leading researchers and institutions worldwide, fostering international knowledge exchange and educational innovation.

Bridging Digital Divides

By providing screen-free access to coding concepts, PlayBot helps bridge digital divides, making computational thinking accessible to children from diverse backgrounds and environments.

Accessibility Initiatives

Our curriculum is designed to be accessible to all children, regardless of socioeconomic background, technological access, or prior exposure to digital learning.

Global Diversity

PlayBot's screen-free approach transcends language and cultural barriers, making computational thinking and coding accessible to children around the world.

Community Outreach

Through strategic partnerships with community organizations and schools, PlayBot reaches children in underserved communities, ensuring that all children have opportunities to develop essential 21st-century skills.

PlayBot 的全球影響力

我們的課程正在對全球幼兒教育產生積極影響。

教育創新

PlayBot 正在革新幼兒教育中計算思維的教學方式，目前已在亞洲超過 50 所學校實施，並引起全球越來越多的關注。

研究貢獻

我們的研究成果已在國際會議上展示並發表於主要學術期刊，推動了全球幼兒科技教育領域的發展。

消除數字鴻溝

通過提供無屏幕的編碼概念學習方式，PlayBot 幫助消除數字鴻溝，使來自不同背景和環境的兒童都能接觸到計算思維教育。
