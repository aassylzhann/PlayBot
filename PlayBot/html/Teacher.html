我们的老师

Comprehensive Training Materials

  • Detailed Lesson Plans：Lesson plans are written step - by - step, with clear classification of teaching aids, such as using rubber bands to distinguish materials for different lessons.
  • High - Quality Teaching Aids：High - quality teaching aids are provided, including map pictures and robot operation guides, to help teachers quickly master the teaching content.
  • Adequate Training：There are PowerPoint explanations and hands - on sessions, allowing teachers to experience robot operation, like learning to control the movement and functions of the robot.

全方位培训资料

  • 教案详尽：教案按步骤详细编写，还配备清晰的教具分类，像用橡皮筋区分不同课程所需物料。
  • 教具优质：提供高质量的教具，例如地图图片和机器人操作指南，助力教师快速掌握教学内容。
  • 培训充足：有 PowerPoint 讲解和实际操作环节，让教师能亲身体验机器人操作，比如学习机器人的移动和功能设置。

Practical Operation Opportunities

  • Training Course Experience：Teachers participate in training courses before the project starts, where they can actually operate the robot, such as learning to control its movement and functions.
  • Independent Pre - operation：Teachers try to operate the map and robot on their own before formal teaching, planning teaching paths to prepare well for the lessons.

实践操作机会

  • 培训课程体验：在项目开始前，教师会参加培训课程，实际操作机器人，例如学习控制机器人的移动和功能设置。
  • 自主预操作：教师在正式教学前，会自行尝试操作地图和机器人，规划教学路径，为教学做好充分准备。

Continuous Support System

  • Immediate Problem - Solving：When teachers encounter problems like complex map operations during teaching, relevant personnel provide timely support, such as sending operation videos.
  • Professional Resource Sharing：The project team shares additional teaching resources, such as operation videos for complex maps, to assist teachers in teaching better.

持续支持体系

  • 问题即时解决：当教师在教学中遇到复杂地图操作等问题时，会有相关人员通过发送操作视频等方式及时提供支持。
  • 专业资源共享：项目组会分享额外的教学资源，如复杂地图的操作视频，帮助教师更好地开展教学。

Integration of Innovative Technology

  • Expert Leadership：The project is led by professors with rich experience in innovative technology, providing professional guidance and inspiration to teachers.
  • Interdisciplinary Integration：Programming is combined with other disciplines, such as Chinese and mathematics, broadening teachers' teaching perspectives.

创新科技融合

  • 专家引领：由在创新科技领域经验丰富的教授负责项目，为教师提供专业的指导和启发。
  • 跨学科整合：将编程与其他学科领域，如语文、数学等相结合，拓宽教师的教学视野。
