Teacher Training Features

Comprehensive Training Materials

Detailed Lesson Plans

Lesson plans are written step - by - step, with clear classification of teaching aids, such as using rubber bands to distinguish materials for different lessons.

High - Quality Teaching Aids

High - quality teaching aids are provided, including map pictures and robot operation guides, to help teachers quickly master the teaching content.

Adequate Training

There are PowerPoint explanations and hands - on sessions, allowing teachers to experience robot operation, like learning to control the movement and functions of the robot.

Practical Operation Opportunities

Training Course Experience

Teachers participate in training courses before the project starts, where they can actually operate the robot, such as learning to control its movement and functions.

Independent Pre - operation

Teachers try to operate the map and robot on their own before formal teaching, planning teaching paths to prepare well for the lessons.

Continuous Support System

Immediate Problem - Solving

When teachers encounter problems like complex map operations during teaching, relevant personnel provide timely support, such as sending operation videos.

Professional Resource Sharing

The project team shares additional teaching resources, such as operation videos for complex maps, to assist teachers in teaching better.

Integration of Innovative Technology

Expert Leadership

The project is led by professors with rich experience in innovative technology, providing professional guidance and inspiration to teachers.

Interdisciplinary Integration

Programming is combined with other disciplines, such as Chinese and mathematics, broadening teachers' teaching perspectives.

教師培訓特點

全方位培訓資料

教案詳盡

教案按步驟詳細編寫，還配備清晰的教具分類，像用橡皮筋區分不同課程所需物料。

教具優質

提供高品質的教具，例如地圖圖片和機器人操作指南，助力教師快速掌握教學內容。

培訓充足

有PowerPoint講解和實際操作環節，讓教師能親身體驗機器人操作，比如學習機器人的移動和功能設置。

實踐操作機會

培訓課程體驗

在項目開始前，教師會參加培訓課程，實際操作機器人，例如學習控制機器人的移動和功能設置。

自主預操作

教師在正式教學前，會自行嘗試操作地圖和機器人，規劃教學路徑，為教學做好充分準備。

持續支持體系

問題即時解决

當教師在教學中遇到複雜地圖操作等問題時，會有相關人員通過發送操作視頻等管道及時提供支援。

專業資源共亯

項目組會分享額外的教學資源，如複雜地圖的操作視頻，幫助教師更好地開展教學。

創新科技融合

專家引領

由在創新科技領域經驗豐富的教授負責項目，為教師提供專業的指導和啟發。

跨學科綜合

將程式設計與其他學科領域，如語文、數學等相結合，拓寬教師的教學視野。
