Shaping the Future of Early Childhood Education through Creative Learning

通過創造性學習塑造幼稚教育的未來

Mission

Balance a creative learning ecosystem driven by technology and traditional tools, cultivate future citizens with computational thinking, creativity, and perseverance, and enable every child to express, create, and collaborate confidently in the AI era.

Vision

Become a global leader in AI + education innovation platforms, reshape learning environments, and make technology a catalyst for children's无限 potential, not a replacement for human intelligence.

使命

平衡科技與傳統工具驅動的創意學習生態，培養具備 計算思維、創造力與堅毅品質, 的未來公民，讓每個兒童都能在AI時代自信地表達、創造與合作。

願景

成為全球領先的AI +教育創新平臺，重構學習場景，使科技成為激發兒童無限潜能的催化劑，而非替代人類智慧的工具。

Learning Objectives: SCALE

Based on the core literacy of the AI era, we define five educational objectives (SCALE):

Scientific Literacy

Use AI to support scientific inquiry, guide children to actively explore scientific principles through the "questioning - experimenting - verifying" cycle. This practice helps them construct early scientific thinking frameworks and enhances both scientific cognition and practical skills.

Computational Thinking

Utilize robot programming tools to teach children how to decompose problems, design algorithms, and debug/optimize solutions, fostering the development of logical thinking frameworks.

AI Literacy Integration

Embed AI-driven interactive dialogues into creative learning pathways, supported by AI-powered analysis of learning data. Through visualized explanations of natural language processing and visual recognition principles, children gain deeper AI understanding and cultivate digital core literacy.

Learning to Learn / Metacognition

Combine AI-generated content (AIGC) with embodied creation (e.g., robot programming, maker activities) through task-driven challenges and reflective cycles. By iterating through "planning - execution - review" processes, learners master cross-disciplinary innovation methods while developing self-regulated learning abilities, transcending traditional educational boundaries.

Engineering & Design Thinking

Prioritize empathy by designing simulated social scenarios (e.g., interactive smart pets). This guides children to establish foundational Engineering Design Process (EDP) frameworks while solving real-world problems, strengthening their understanding of human society and achieving dual cultivation of engineering innovation and humanistic values.

教育目标：SCAL

基于 AI 时代核心素养，定义五大教育目标（SCALE）

科學素養

借助AI支持科學探究，引導兒童主動探究科學原理，在“提問-實驗-驗證”的實踐中，逐步構建早期的科學思維框架，提升科學認知與實踐能力。

計算思維

借助機器人程式設計工具，引導兒童學習分解問題、設計算灋、調試優化，建立邏輯思維框架。

AI素養融合

通過AI智慧解析兒童學習數據，在創意學習路徑中嵌入AI對話互動，通過自然語言處理和視覺識別原理的視覺化，幫助兒童更深入地認識AI，培養數位時代覈心素養。

學會學習與元認知

融合AI生成科技（AIGC）與具身創作（如機器人程式設計、創客活動），通過任務驅動與反思閉環，引導兒童在科技實踐中建立元認知策略。 學習者通過“規劃-執行-複盤”的反覆運算流程，既能掌握跨學科創新方法，更能在「科技賦能」中逐步形成自我調節學習能力，突破傳統學習邊界

工程與設計思維

將同理心（empathy）作為首要環節，通過創設智慧寵物互動等類比社交場景，引導兒童在解决問題的過程中，幫助兒童建立工程設計的基礎思維框架EDP，强化了對人類社會的理解與尊重，實現工程創新與人文關懷的雙重培養

Teaching Learning Principals

· Child Agency-Driven "Learning by Doing" Model

Guided by project-based learning (PBL), children apply knowledge to solve real-world problems. This approach emphasizes children’s agency in the learning process, encouraging hands-on exploration. Through PBL, they identify, analyze, and resolve challenges in practical contexts, achieving true knowledge internalization and application.

· AI Is Understandable and Learnable

AI tools (e.g., smart drawing tools, voice assistants) provide heuristic guidance while preserving children’s autonomy in decision-making. Children learn foundational AI principles through accessible methods and develop skills to collaborate with AI effectively.

· Boundaryless Learning Through Interdisciplinary Integration

Break down disciplinary silos with integrated AI + programming + maker courses, nurturing children’s creativity, real-world problem-solving skills, and perseverance.

· Social-Emotional Learning

Learning is inherently social. Through collaborative projects (child-AI or peer teamwork), children deepen cognition via interaction. Communication, role division, and social engagement foster self-awareness, teamwork, knowledge internalization, emotional regulation, and meaning-making.

創意學習原則

· 以兒童主體能動性（child agency）驅動的“做中學”模式

通過項目制學習（PBL）引導兒童在解决真實問題中應用知識。 強調兒童在學習過程中的主體地位，鼓勵他們通過動手實踐探索知識，同時借助項目制學習，讓兒童在實際操作中發現問題、分析問題並解决問題，真正實現知識的內化與運用。

· AI是可以被理解和學習的

AI工具（如智慧畫筆、語音助手）僅提供啟發式建議，保留兒童自主決策空間。 兒童可以通過有效的管道理解AI的基本原理，學會與AI協同工作。

· 跨學科融合的“無邊界學習”

打破學科壁壘，設計AI +程式設計+創客課程，培養兒童的創造力、真實問題解决能力和堅持性。

· 社會情感學習

學習的“社會性”本質強調個體在互動與合作中深化認知。 通過兒童-AI或同伴合作完成項目任務，在溝通與分工中培養自我意識與團隊合作能力，在社會互動中實現知識內化、情緒調控與意義生成。

Educator Requirements

Creative Instructional Design Skills

Use open-ended AI challenges in projects (e.g., “How can AI reduce food waste?”) to guide children’s self-directed exploration.

Interdisciplinary Project Teaching Skills

Integrate cross-disciplinary knowledge into teaching activities, enabling children to connect concepts across domains through vivid and engaging methods, helping them build effective learning capabilities.

Ethical Guidance & Emotional Support

Incorporate ethical discussions into the use of technological tools (such as "What if AI generates wrong answers?") to guide children to think dialectically about the role of technology and their relationship with it. Pay attention to children's emotional changes during learning to prevent technology dependence or frustration, and cultivate collaboration and communication skills, strengthening the awareness that "technology is a tool, and humans have more creativity.".

教育者要求

創意教學設計能力

在項目中預留AI未解决的開放性問題（如“如何用AI减少食物浪費”），引導兒童自主探索。

跨學科項目教學能力

注重將跨學科知識融入教學活動，讓兒童能够以生動有趣的管道，將不同領域的知識串聯起來，幫助其建立起有效的學習能力。

倫理引導與情感支持

在科技工具使用中滲透倫理討論（如“如果AI生成錯誤的答案，怎麼辦？ ”），引導兒童辯證思考科技的作用與自身的關係。 關注兒童在學習中的情緒變化，避免科技依賴或挫敗感，培養合作性及溝通能力，强化“科技是工具，人有更多創意”的認知。
