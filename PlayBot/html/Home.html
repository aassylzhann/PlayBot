    Introduction

    This curriculum was born out of a vision to empower young learners, providing them with the rich, hands-on learning experiences that are essential to navigating the ever-evolving trends and requirements of the 21st century.

    In creating the PlayBot curriculum, our goal was to prepare our children for the future by fostering creativity, nurturing computational thinking, and building vital problem-solving and social skills through engaging, playful activities.

    Our commitment to safety and durability is also a cornerstone of the PlayBot curriculum. The coding robot embedded in the PlayBot curriculum has been carefully engineered to be safe, sturdy, and fall resistant.

    Key features include:

    • Engagement:Utilizes consistent color-coded indicators that mirror the command buttons
    • Ease of Use: Designed for intuitive play—plan the route, press the command buttons to code the robot
    • Screen-free Design: Suitable for preschoolers with age-appropriate command buttons
    • Interactive Learning: Introduces basic coding concepts through contextualized play activities

    序言

    本人深感榮幸為《PlayBot課程》撰寫此序言。這套突破性的教育課程以嶄新理念將遊戲與無屏幕編程及機械人技術巧妙融合，源於一個願景——透過豐富的實體操作學習體驗，賦能新生代應對21世紀瞬息萬變的趨勢與挑戰。

    研發PlayBot課程的初衷，在於以寓教於樂的活動培育兒童創造力、運算思維、解難能力及社交技巧，為孩子裝備未來所需的關鍵技能。

    安全性與耐用性亦是PlayBot課程的重要基石。課程專用編程機械人經精心設計，具備防摔抗震特性。

    主要特色包括：

    • 增強參與度：採用與指令鍵同色系的流程指示燈，簡化程序追蹤與除錯
    • 簡易操作：直觀三步曲——規劃路線、輸入指令、啟動執行
    • 無屏幕設計：配備符合學前兒童操作能力的指令按鍵
    • 互動學習： 透過情境化遊戲活動導入基礎編程概念
