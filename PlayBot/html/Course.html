PlayBot Curriculum

Our flagship curriculum designed for intuitive learning through play and exploration. Children develop computational thinking and programming skills through engaging with robots.

Beginner Level

Introduction to robot programming concepts through guided play. Children learn basic commands and sequencing through interactive activities.

  • Basic robot commands
  • Simple sequences
  • Introduction to loops
  • Problem-solving basics

Intermediate Level

Building on the foundations, children explore more complex programming concepts including conditionals and functions.

  • Conditional statements
  • Advanced loops
  • Function creation
  • Robot sensor integration

Advanced Level

Mastering complex programming concepts and applying them to solve real-world problems with robot programming.

  • Project-based challenges
  • Complex algorithms
  • Robot customization
  • Collaborative problem-solving

PlayBot 課程

我們的旗艦課程，專為通過遊戲和探索進行直觀學習而設計。兒童通過與機器人互動，培養計算思維和編程技能。

Tinker Maker Curriculum

A hands-on curriculum focused on creativity and engineering. Children learn to design, build, and program their own robotic creations while developing critical thinking skills.

Beginner Level

Introduction to basic engineering concepts and simple mechanisms. Children learn to build and program simple robotic structures.

  • Basic engineering concepts
  • Simple mechanisms
  • Introduction to sensors
  • Creative problem-solving

Intermediate Level

Advancing to more complex builds and programming challenges. Children create robots that can interact with their environment.

  • Advanced mechanisms
  • Sensor integration
  • Interactive robots
  • Design challenges

Advanced Level

Creating sophisticated robotic solutions to solve complex problems. Children design, build, and program robots to address real-world challenges.

  • Complex robotic systems
  • Innovation challenges
  • Engineering principles
  • Project management

TinkerMaker 課程

注重創造力和工程學的實踐課程。兒童在培養批判性思維技能的同時，學習設計、建造和編程自己的機器人創作。

MACO is an innovative curriculum for teaching robot programming through play, developed at The Education University of Hong Kong.
