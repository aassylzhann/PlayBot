About PlayBot

PlayBot is an innovative early childhood curriculum that empowers children to develop computational thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills through playful, screen-free robotics and coding activities. Our mission is to balance technology and traditional tools, cultivating future-ready learners who can express, create, and collaborate confidently in the AI era.

關於 PlayBot

PlayBot 是一套創新的幼兒課程，通過遊戲化、無屏幕的機器人編程活動，培養兒童的計算思維、創造力和解決問題能力。我們的使命是平衡科技與傳統工具，培養能在 AI 時代自信表達、創造與合作的未來人才。

Our Philosophy

Rooted in the "learning through play" philosophy, PlayBot integrates storytelling, hands-on exploration, and cross-curricular challenges. Children learn by doing—solving real-world problems, collaborating with peers, and engaging in creative design. Our approach is inclusive and accessible, making computational thinking and coding available to all children, regardless of background.

課程理念

PlayBot 堅持「做中學」的理念，融合故事講述、動手探索和跨學科挑戰。孩子們在實踐中學習，解決真實問題，與同伴合作，發揮創意設計。我們注重包容與可及性，讓所有兒童都能接觸到計算思維與編程教育。

Curriculum Overview

PlayBot blends playful learning with screen-free coding and robotics for young children. The curriculum is aligned with international standards and the Hong Kong Education Bureau's "joyful learning through play" philosophy. Activities are designed to foster computational thinking, creativity, and collaboration through hands-on, age-appropriate challenges.

  • Screen-free coding robots for ages 3-5
  • Interactive voice feedback and storytelling
  • Cross-curricular maps and creative problem-solving
  • Inclusive, accessible, and culturally adaptable

課程概述

PlayBot 結合遊戲學習與無屏幕編程和機器人技術，課程對標國際標準及香港教育局「愉快學習，遊戲中成長」理念。所有活動均以動手實踐、年齡適宜的挑戰，培養兒童的計算思維、創造力與合作能力。

  • 適合 3-5 歲兒童的無屏幕編程機器人
  • 互動語音反饋與故事講述
  • 跨學科地圖與創意解難
  • 包容性強，適用於不同文化背景

Global Impact

PlayBot has reached over 50 schools and 1,000+ children across 5 countries, making computational thinking accessible to diverse communities. Our curriculum bridges digital divides by providing screen-free, hands-on learning, and has been recognized in international research and educational innovation initiatives.

全球影響

PlayBot 已在全球 5 個國家、超過 50 所學校推廣，惠及 1,000 多名兒童，讓不同背景的孩子都能接觸到計算思維教育。課程以無屏幕、動手學習方式消除數字鴻溝，並在國際研究與教育創新中獲得認可。

Project Overview

The PlayBot project is supported by the Research Grant Council (RGC) of Hong Kong (General Research Fund 18604423). We collaborate with schools and educators to co-create meaningful learning experiences and advance early childhood technology education.

項目概述

PlayBot 項目獲香港研究資助局（RGC）資助（GRF 項目編號 18604423），並與學校及教育工作者合作，共同創建有意義的學習體驗，推動幼兒科技教育發展。
