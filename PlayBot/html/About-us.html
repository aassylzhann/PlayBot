Robot Programming Through Play

Effects on Kindergarten Children's Engagement and Computational Thinking

應用遊戲教學法於機械人編程活動

對幼兒課堂投入和運算思維的影響

Curriculum Overview

PlayBot is an educational curriculum that blends playful learning with screen-free coding and robotics for young children. Designed in alignment with the K-12 Computer Science Framework and Standards, this program fosters computational thinking through engaging hands-on activities.

Our curriculum is built on a three-dimensional computational thinking framework specifically developed for early childhood education. All activities follow the "joyful learning through play" philosophy of the Hong Kong Education Bureau's Kindergarten Education Curriculum Guide (2017).

PlayBot utilizes the Tale-Bot Pro—a 100% screen-free coding robot for children ages 3-5 that introduces fundamental programming concepts through:

  • Age-appropriate command buttons with no screens
  • Interactive voice feedback for immediate learning
  • Cross-curricular maps and accessories
  • Storytelling and creative problem-solving opportunities

課程概述

PlayBot 是一個教育課程，將遊戲學習與無屏幕編程和機器人技術相結合，專為幼兒設計。本課程符合 K-12 計算機科學框架和標準，通過引人入勝的動手活動培養計算思維。

我們的課程建立在專為幼兒教育開發的三維計算思維框架上。所有活動均遵循香港教育局《幼稚園教育課程指引》(2017) 的「愉快學習，遊戲中成長」理念。

PlayBot 使用 Tale-Bot Pro—一款為 3-5 歲兒童設計的 100% 無屏幕編程機器人，通過以下方式介紹基本編程概念：

  • 適合年齡的指令按鈕，無屏幕設計
  • 互動語音反饋提供即時學習體驗
  • 跨學科地圖和配件
  • 講故事和創造性解決問題的機會
