PlayBot Platform

Our platform uses MatataStudio's Tale Bot Pro Hands-on Coding Set Educational Edition.

Tale-Bot Pro is a cute and smart robot who can walk, draw, sing, dance, speak and record! With the Interactive map, the robot is ready to speak 10 languages, provide an Interactive learning environment and bring more coding fun!

PlayBot 平台

我们的平台使用MatataStudio的Tale Bot Pro动手编码集教育版。

Tale-Bot Pro是一个可爱而聪明的机器人，可以走路、画画、唱歌、跳舞、说话和录音！ 应用交互式地图后，机器人可以说10种语言，提供交互式学习环境，并带来更多的编码乐趣！

Why choose Tale-Bot Pro?

Screen-free design with command buttons

Age-appropriate for preschoolers

Smart interactive technology

With instant voice feedback to provide instructive, interactive and creative learning environment

Voice commands

Develop children’s creativity and storytelling skills through recording function and tell stories

为什么选择Tale-Bot Pro?

无屏幕设计，带命令按钮

适合学龄前儿童的年龄

智能交互技术

通过即时语音反馈，提供有指导性、互动性和创造性的学习环境

语音命令

通过录音功能和讲故事，培养孩子的创造力和讲故事的能力
