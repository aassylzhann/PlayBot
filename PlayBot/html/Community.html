Control group

Teaching method: Direct guided activities

Format:The teacher introduces the operation methods and functions of robots and maps to the children, and leads them to use robots and maps for imaginative activities, including creative elements.

Purpose: By providing direct guidance to help young children systematically learn programming knowledge and skills, we aim to enhance their problem-solving abilities.

Course content:

  • Teacher led class wide activities
  • Clear task guidance and completion
  • Challenge Station Activity
Free play group

Teaching method: Free game activities

Format: Children explore the operation methods and functions of robots and maps on their own, and expect them to use robots and maps for imaginative games, including creative elements.

Purpose: Cultivate children's creativity and self-learning ability through free play, and stimulate their interest in programming.

Course content:

  • Free exploration and creation
  • Multiple map options
  • Emphasize self-directed learning
Guidedplay group

Teaching Method: Guided Game Activities

Format: The instructional game group teacher needs to guide children in learning programming and improving their computational thinking during the process of playing.

Purpose: Developing children's computational thinking through guided games, while paying attention to individual differences, to meet the learning needs of children with different abilities.

Course content:

  • Combining teacher guidance with children's independent exploration
  • Differentiated teaching
  • Diversified teaching resources and tasks
控制组

教学方法: 直接指導式活動

形式:老师向幼儿介绍机器人及地图的操作方式及功能，并带领幼儿利用机器人及地图进行想象活动，并有创作的元素包含在内。

目的:通过直接指导帮助幼儿系统学习编程知识和技能，提升解决问题的能力。

课程内容:

  • 教师主导的全班活动
  • 明确的任务指导与完成
  • 挑战站活动
自由遊戲组

教学方法: 自由遊戲活動

形式:幼儿自行探索机器人及地图的操作方式及功能，期望幼儿利用机器人及地图进行想象游戏，并有创作的元素包含在内。

目的:通过自由游戏培养幼儿的创造力和自主学习能力，激发对编程的兴趣。

课程内容:

  • 自由探索与创作
  • 多种地图选择
  • 强调自主学习
指導组

教学方法: 指導式遊戲活動

形式:指导式游戏组老师需要在幼儿游玩的过程中指导幼儿学习编程及提升其运算思维。

目的:通过指导式游戏培养幼儿的运算思维，同时注重个体差异，满足不同能力幼儿的学习需求。

课程内容:

  • 结合教师引导与幼儿自主探索
  • 差异化教学
  • 多样化的教学资源和任务

MACO is an innovative curriculum for teaching robot programming through play, developed at The Education University of Hong Kong.
